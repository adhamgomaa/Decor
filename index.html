Our Services

We offer top-notch interior , architecture and landscape design services
based on countless years of experience in the industry.

Interior Design & Architecture

Our clients are our major inspiration.. We simply strive to fuse our clients' identity into our innovative interior solutions to come up, each time, with a bespoke unique blend.

Landscape Design

Like music, the landscape affects the soul, the body and the innermost contemplations. We at RSI know how make your landscape - on all scales - your personal in-house heaven..

Commercial Design

Because the best of commercial projects can easily fail if its interior is not just as well, we at RSI do the art of balancing functional needs and aesthetic preferences inside of a structure. After all, the purpose of a building is really defined by its interior, not only its exterior.

FF&E and Styling

Like the soul to the body, styling your space is the way to showcase your choices, interests and even your personality. Accessories & fixtures allow you to inject your personality in your interior through them. We at RSI help you create a story that you would want to potray .
